AKAN NAME CHECKER



Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

MALE NAMES

  • Sunday-Kwasi
  • Monday-Kwadwo
  • Tuesday- Kwabena
  • Wednesday- Kwaku
  • Thursday- Yaw
  • Friday- Kofi
  • Saturday- Kwame

FEMALE NAMES

  • Sunday- Akosua
  • Monday- Adwoa
  • Tuesday- Abenaa
  • Wednesday- Akua
  • Thursday- Yaa
  • Friday- Afua
  • Saturday- Ama
